Previous Works


Volunteer Activities

Since its inception, STEP at B-CC has participated in and facilitated several community service events.

Making Meals for Schools in Africa

On MLK Day 2020, STEP at B-CC helped package over ten thousand meals for impoverished South African school children

Packagining Homeless Care Bags

STEP at B-CC packaged over one hundred twenty care bags to distribute to homeless individuals in Montgomery County.

Assembling Women's Hygiene Kits

STEP at B-CC packaged women's hygiene kits for local homeless shelters.


Advocacy Work

After suspending in-person activities due to the COVID-19 pandemic, STEP spearheaded a lobbying initiative to support and advocate for the Blueprint for Maryland's Future Act. In collaboration with Strong Schools Maryland, STEP was able to launch a letter writing campaign, provide important testimony, and mobilize students across Maryland to join the initiative.

Letter-writing Campaign

Organized a letter-writing campaign, sending over 800 emails from students across Maryland to members of the state general assembly in support of the bill.

Testimony to Montgomery County’s State Delegation

Testified in front of the full Montgomery County Delegation asking for their support on the bill.

Mobilized Students To Lobby their own Legislators

Recruited and trained students across Maryland to hold virtual meetings with their elected officials to advocate for their support of the bill. By creating a lobbying brief that highlighted the main idea, key arguments, and counter points for the bill, STEP taught students about the bill, and how to present their arguments to elected officials.
